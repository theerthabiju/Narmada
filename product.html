White Puttu Podi

Premium quality Puttu Podi made from carefully selected rice, finely ground to deliver soft and fluffy puttu every time. Hygienically processed to ensure authentic taste and freshness.

White Puttu is one of the most cherished traditional breakfast dishes of Kerala, loved for generations for its simplicity and wholesome taste. Made from finely ground white rice flour and gently steamed in cylindrical molds, puttu has long been a staple in Kerala households.

Historically, puttu has been a part of daily life in South Indian kitchens, especially in Kerala, where it is commonly served with ripe bananas, kadala curry, or coconut. The dish reflects the region's rich agricultural heritage, where rice and coconut have always been essential ingredients.
