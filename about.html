About

Narmada

Bringing Authentic Kerala
Taste to Your Kitchen

Narmada Products began importing frozen foods to North America in 2003 under Margin Free VS Inc. In 2008, Narmada Foods India was established to export authentic Kerala food products, while Narmada Products Inc. manages U.S. distribution.
In 2022, the company expanded its operations in Alappuzha, India, bringing production, packaging, and freezing in-house. Narmada offers ready-to-eat foods, snacks, spices, pickles, flours, and frozen fish, all produced with strict quality and safety standards.
The company also introduced Shaj’s Signature, a premium brand featuring rice, rice products, and snacks.
Narmada products are now available in major Indian stores and restaurants across the USA.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver high-quality, hygienically prepared food products that preserve the authentic taste of Kerala tradition. We focus on using carefully selected ingredients, modern processing methods, and strict quality control to ensure every product meets the highest standards of safety and freshness.

We are committed to making traditional cooking simple, convenient, and accessible for families, retailers, and food businesses around the world.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a globally recognized food brand known for quality, trust, and authentic taste. We aim to expand our presence across international markets while maintaining the traditional values that define our identity.

Through continuous innovation, sustainable practices, and strong customer relationships, we strive to set new standards in the food industry while delivering excellence in every product.
